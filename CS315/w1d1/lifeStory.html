My life story

My name is Khelifa Ahmed. I am originally from Ethiopia. I grew up in Addis Ababa, which is the capital city of Ethiopia. As a kid, I enjoyed playing soccer on streets on which a vehicle travels on. It was always fun when all the neighborhood kids gather and play together. It left me a great memory of childhood. I grew up with a family of eight. I have brothers and sisters in Ethiopia.

I also have some brothers and sisters in the United States of America. I have been living with my family in Maryland for a couple of years. I studied Biology at Morgan State University in Baltimore, Maryland. I made so many friends at Morgan State University.
